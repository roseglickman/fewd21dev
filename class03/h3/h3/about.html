Our Story

Like many, we began as a start-up business. Our founder Luka Jalmari Draganov wanted to automate the print estimating and basic accounting process in her own business. She worked with a team of Newcastle University students to develop software to manage this more efficiently. Luka realised that other individuals and start up companies could also benefit from this and formed Relaxr in 1981 to begin selling the software. As desktop PCs became widespread, so the Relaxr range grew and our business went from strength to strength. We floated on the London Stock Exchange in 1989 and in 1999 we entered the FTSE 100 where we still remain. We understand our customers needs and the challenges they face. Like them, Relaxr has innovation and entrepreneurial spirit at heart. Today we have millions of customers and circa 14,000 employees across 24 countries, including UK and Ireland, mainland Europe, North America, South Africa, Australia, Asia and Brazil. All helping individuals increase their work efficiency.
